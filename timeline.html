The seed

August 2023

The seeds for BUILD Beyond were sown on August 17th, 2023. That night, over 20 BUILD alumni, staff, and mentors gathered at Stillwater restaurant in downtown Boston, to celebrate the start of a new college term. After a good feast and great conversations, veteran alumni brought down the house with an impromptu panel of advice to the rising college freshmen. And soon a new alumni community would take root.

The working group

Fall 2024

In Fall 2024, a grassroots working group of BUILD alumni, staff, partners, and board members came together to answer an important question: How can we better recruit, engage, and support BUILD alumni while keeping youth at the center? After several months of collaboration, the group proposed a solution: create a club by alumni, for alumni, designed to connect them with opportunities through BUILD's networks for career success and entrepreneurship.

Kickoff

January 2025

BUILD Beyond officially began its journey with a powerful kickoff event, an afterparty following the BUILD Winter PitchFest. Jeremy Redburn, Co-founder of Salsify, shared invaluable insights on business development, networking, and career growth. So inspired by our members? energy and drive, he has since become an active mentor and ongoing supporter of the club.

Panel

May 1 2025

BUILD Beyond marked a major milestone with its first large-scale panel hosted at Underscore VC, led by our newly elected executive board. Surrounded by seasoned venture capitalists, our members gained first-hand exposure to the world of VC, sparking deeper connections and a stronger sense of purpose across our growing community.

BUILDFest

May 27 2025

BUILDFest is the annual showcase and celebration where students, alumni and community members come together to watch Y1 students pitch, share stories, and reconnect. It?s part expo, part reunion and all about highlighting the creativity and growth within the BUILD Boston.
